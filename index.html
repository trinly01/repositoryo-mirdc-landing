What is Repositoryo?

A web-based and access-controlled information system that provides a centralized repository of any information in different formats. It aims to collect data, consolidate and transform into informative reports that any top management would love to see. Repositoryo is your modern File System designed to be responsive and user-friendly in managing your information assets. It has a Form Builder feature to build electronic forms as a tool to collect and process data or sign digitally and automate business processes with its built-in data analytics.

Form and WorkFlow Automation

Sign and Approve documents digitally and create powerful forms to speed up the your business processes reducing the slow approval of documents

Digitize Documents

Upload your old documents and never waste your time again by finding your misfiled document and don't recreate the lost information... Our AI can extract Data from your documents in any format that can be indexed to its built-in Search Engine

Document Control

Manage and secure documents relating to the implementation, maintenance, and versions for continuous improvement of a management system that is aligned and compliant to QMS and ISMS

Document Tracking

Monitor both incoming and outgoing documents
integrated with bar coding system for efficient tracking.

  • Reduce lost and misfiled documents.
  • Provide faster search and retrieval of documents.

  • Streamline information and workflow.
Security-first by design

Set a role-based access control or role-based security to your information assets restricting system access to your intended recipients or copy holders.
